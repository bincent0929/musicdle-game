How To Play

Here's some info about the game before you start.

🎧 More song with every guess: You start with 6 seconds of song. Every guess grants you 6 more seconds of the song for you to guess from (you get a max of 30 seconds).

🎯 Smarter guesses: Type an artist’s name or the name of the song in the search bar, and you'll see a variety of choices you can choose from.

🪄 If you just don't know it: You can use the Reveal button to show you the answer, but you lose the game. You should only use it when you really aren't sure what the song is.

Press the arrow to start playing! Refresh the page to get these tips again.
