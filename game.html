How To Play

A quick goon before you dive into today’s track.

🎧 Unlock time: You start with 6 seconds of audio. Every wrong guess grants +6 seconds (max 30s).

🎯 Smarter guesses: Type an artist’s name to browse their songs in the dropdown, then tap the best match.

🪄 Reveal: Stuck? Reveal shows the answer but ends the round—save it for when you really need it.

🏁 Finish later: The Finish button appears after a reveal so you can review or restart.

Tap the arrow to start playing. You can reopen these tips with a refresh.
